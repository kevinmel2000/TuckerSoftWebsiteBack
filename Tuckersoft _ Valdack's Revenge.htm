Based on the popular Space Fleet program, you must battle with Valdack, save your trusty crew, and protect the USS Callister. Hip Hip Hooray!

In this Tuckersoft hit, YOU get to call the shots as Captain Robert Daly. The lives of Walton, Cole, Packer and the rest of the crew are in your hands. Beware, this time Valdack knows exactly how to use the power of the crystal.

The people, places and actions depicted in this game are based on true events.
